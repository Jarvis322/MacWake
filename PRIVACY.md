# Privacy Policy — Wake

_Last updated: 24 June 2026_

Wake (“the app”) is a battery and power monitoring utility for macOS. Your
privacy is simple to describe: **the app collects nothing.**

## Data we collect
None. Wake does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. It has
no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.

## Data stored on your device
Wake stores its settings and battery/session history locally inside its own
sandboxed application container on your Mac. This data never leaves your device
and is removed when you delete the app.

## Network
Wake does not make network connections and does not send any information to us
or to any third party.

## Permissions
Wake may ask for permission to show local notifications, used only to alert you
about fast battery drain or high battery temperature. You can revoke this at any
time in System Settings → Notifications. Battery and power readings use standard,
non-privileged macOS APIs and require no special permission.
