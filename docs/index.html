Dynamic Island

Lives in your notch.

On notch MacBooks, MacWake blends into the hardware. Hover to expand — live power, battery health, temperatures, and a JARVIS-style arc reactor that pulses with your charging power.

Features

Everything your battery wants you to know.

🔋

Charge Limit

Cap charging at 50–95% to cut long-term wear, across the full Apple Silicon M-series. Picks the best method per chip automatically.

⛵

Sailing & Calibration

Drift the battery in a band instead of micro-charging, plus a deep calibration cycle (discharge → full charge → hold) to keep the gauge accurate.

⌨️

Command Line

Install the macwake CLI and control charging from Terminal — status, charging, adapter, energy, fan.

⚡️

Energy Mode

Switch macOS Energy Mode — Automatic, Low Power, or High Power — straight from the menu.

🌀

Manual Fan Speed

On Macs with fans, set a target RPM. Reverts to automatic above 92°C as a safety failsafe. Experimental.

🏝️

JARVIS Dynamic Island

On notch Macs, an arc-reactor HUD with rotating rings and a core that pulses with charging power.

🎛️

Custom Menu Bar

Show exactly what you want — icon, battery %, power, time remaining, temperature — with a live preview.

🌡️

Temperature Alerts

Notified when battery temperature climbs past 42°C — before sustained heat shortens capacity.

📈

Health Decay Log

A timeline of your battery's maximum capacity. Watch the trend and know when to act.

💨

Fast Drain Detection

Catches sudden 5%+ drops after wake-from-sleep and alerts you to runaway processes.

🔌

Plugged In All Day

Staying at 100% for 24+ hours degrades lithium. MacWake warns you before it adds up.

🌍

English & Turkish

Full localization that automatically follows your macOS system language.

🔒

100% Private

No cloud, no account. Your battery data never leaves your Mac.
